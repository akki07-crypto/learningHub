How to Generate your Formspree Endpoint ID
  1. Go to Formspree.io and create a free account (50 free submissions/month).
  2. Click + New Form and set your notification Gmail address.
  3. Copy your Form Endpoint ID (e.g. xpzgqvwb).
  4. Open your .env file in c:\new one\.env and paste your ID: 
    FORMSPREE_ENDPOINT_ID=your_formspree_id_here
